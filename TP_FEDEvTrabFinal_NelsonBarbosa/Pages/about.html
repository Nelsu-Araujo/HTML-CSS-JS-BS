1992-1997

It was on a sunny day in the summer of 1992 that the good news was announced: the birth of the author of Le Negazine, Nelson Filipe Araújo Barbosa, weighing a healthy 4.2kg, in a labour of little shouting and much joy!

1998-2003

The author's childhood was extremely happy! According to him, "living in a rural and bucolic area allowed me to enjoy the simple and beautiful things in our lives: the countryside, the animals, the river, the smell of blooming flowers..."

2004-2009

Education has always played a decisive role in the author's family, and it was also at school that he developed part of his outgoing and communicative personality, contributing greatly to the person he is today.

2010-2015

University, Erasmus in Milan, a short stay in Coimbra... probably the most unforgettable years of the author's life are in this period. Affirmation and liberation catapulted the author into paradise and full confidence!

2016-2021

Entering the world of work in line with the country's situation, where the generation on the dole struggles to survive, was something of a shock for the author, who learnt even more to value his work and qualities!

2022-PRESENT

Here we are! It's me, Nelson! Life takes many turns and it's certainly never too late for new challenges and learning! Looking forward to tomorrow? Yes, but with renewed confidence, aware that I'm already a winner!
