PREFACE BY NELSON ARAÚJO BARBOSA

Le Negazine: a mix of themes

Dear reader, welcome to Le Negazine, a space that honours the traditional written press by providing a thematic upgrade - more specifically on the life of its author, Nelson Araújo Barbosa. A newspaper-magazine, a biographical landmark, a source of ideas for the more curious and, as a last resort, a hobby just to pass the time.

Few people know about the author's short period at the University of Coimbra, where he attended pure journalism classes, the kind you don't see nowadays, you know? His love for writing and reading led to this adventure which, although short, moulded part of Nelson's critical and observant personality.

There's no denying the contribution of digitalisation these days, but it's inevitable to think about the damaging effects it has had on areas of activity such as journalism - and more specifically traditional journalism. Are we witnessing the end of the paper newspaper? Where is the legacy, the history? Only time will tell...
